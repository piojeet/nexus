How it works?

Bring stories to life with the magic of your voice.

At Story on the Go, we harness advanced AI technology to clone your voice, allowing your child to hear stories narrated by you—even when you're not around. This creates a comforting and personalized storytelling experience, strengthening the bond between parent and child.

01

Record Your Voice

Use our intuitive tool to capture a short sample of your voice.

02

AI Voice Cloning

Our AI analyzes and replicates your voice's nuances to create a natural-sounding clone.

03

Personalized Storytelling

Choose or create stories, and our system will narrate them using your cloned voice, making each tale uniquely yours.
